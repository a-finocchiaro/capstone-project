Performance Issues

Performance measures are to be determined after implementation of the District CEDS.

The CEDS must contain a section that lists the performance measures used to evaluate the Planning Organization's successful development and implementation of the CEDS, including but not limited to the:

  • Number of jobs created after the implementation of our CEDS;
  • Number and types of investments undertaken in our region;
  • Number of jobs retained in our region;
  • Amount of private sector investment in the region after implementation of our CEDS, and;
  • Changes in the economic environment of our region.

Source: U.S. Economic Development Administration (2019)

Using KPIs identified for each of the objectives above, our EDD will create and refine an operational logic model to ensure that our organization can efficiently and accurately leverage and track economic development activity across multiple stakeholders.

Strategic Objective:

a. Analyze our regional economy -

Key Performance Indicator (KPI):

  • Number of responses: CEDS Community Survey
  • Number of volunteers: CEDS Strategy Committee
  • Number of priority projects that progress

b. Define regional economic development goals -

  • Number of new members that represent previously underrepresented industry & demographic members
  • Frequency of Board meetings & EDD updates, including outside specialists
  • Number of convening & summit co-sponsors and value of sponsorships

c. Determine regional economic development opportunities -

  • Number of registrants & attendees
  • Action items informed by conferences, retreats, and the AAED Spring/Fall Forum
  • Number of regional activities supported by EDD membership and staff

d. Formulate and implement a regional economic development program -

  • Number of economic development education programs & number of attendees
  • Number of economic development meetings, number of attendees, & diversity of industries and communities served
  • Number of economic resilience-based workshops
