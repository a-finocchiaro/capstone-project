Alan Pruitt is serving the District since 2007. He is a seasoned non-profit executive leader and a graduate of the University of Phoenix. Alan also completed the Thunderbird School of Global Management Executive Certificate in Social and Voluntary Sector Leadership. Alan also completed the International Economic Development Council (IEDC) - Arizona Basic Economic Development Course, and National Business Incubation Association (NBIA) - Incubator Management Certificate Program. In 2019, he served as a US representative to the Americas Competitiveness Exchange (ACE-12) in Santiago de Chile. ACE is the premier economic development, innovation, and entrepreneurial network of the Americas.
